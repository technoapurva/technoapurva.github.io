About Me

Trying to make machines learn

I am ‘Techno’ Apurva Pathak, a data nerd. I am a graduate student at University of California, San Diego where I am working on building a career specialized in machine learning and data science. I have worked for Microsoft as a Software Engineer in the past.

My research background is a testament of my appetite for exploring the field of machine learning. I have carried out extensive research in increasing diversity in recommendation systems, and have published and presented multiple research papers in renowned conferences and seminars.

I enjoy taking part in coding competitions. Along with participating in and winning several robotics and coding events at institute and at national level, I participated in the Conference on Data Sciences 2015 Data Challenge and was the first runner up.

I am enthusiastic about learning new things and acquiring various skills. I utilize my spare time working on cool projects and equipping myself with latest technologies in market. You can follow me on Github to explore the projects that I have worked on or I am working on. Playing musical instruments (guitar, harmonica), roller-skating and playing table tennis are among my other hobbies.
